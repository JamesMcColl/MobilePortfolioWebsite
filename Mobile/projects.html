Grudius X

Grudius X is a small side-scrolling shooter I created for my Introduction to Graphics Programming class. The game was coded entirely in Python. This project was built upon every week of the class and was completed on the last week.

Achimota Centre Project

This project is the largest and most important one I have worked on while in this program. Me and my team of 7 other classmates are tasked with helping the Achimota Center develop a system that will streamline their report creation and allow more quality time helping the children. While the exact details have not been finalized as of yet, this project will be a great test, requiring two whole semesters of work to complete it.
